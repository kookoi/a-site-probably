Important Things to Keep in Mind

  • To contact me email me at Kookoiart@gmail.com. Do not DM me when first asking for a commission! I may lose track of your message! You may Dm me afterwards to ask about progress however.
  • All Payments will be recieved through paypal. I will send an invoice that must be paid within a week. You may pay in small amounts if you need to, but I will only start once it is paid in full.
  • Please provide a visual reference for your character! Don't be afraid to include extra details about a character's personality! It helps with my workflow.
  • Finally I will make sure to show you the initial sketch before moving onto the final product. Make sure to ask for any changes to be made during this step!
